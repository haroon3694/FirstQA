Delivery model
FirstQA Systems follows global delivery model to deliver managed services to our enterprise clients. We leverage our onsite teams in global locations with offshore team members in India to deliver end to end solutions and services. Our projects adopt Waterfall or Agile software development methodology based on project needs. Waterfall is an excellent methodology when business requirements are thoroughly and accurately defined. We’ve had great success using this method to build large and complex applications for our enterprise clients. Agile software development has quickly proven its value in delivering high-quality applications that our client needs. FirstQA’s Agile developers produce superior code that is easier to maintain, adapt and extend. Our agile development practices, such as frequent deliveries, continuous integration, and build, deployment, and tests, reduce risk and rework. Majority of our digital transformation and blockchain projects follow agile model.
